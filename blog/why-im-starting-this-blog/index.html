Why I’m Starting This Blog

July 2026 · 3 min read

If you’ve ever scrolled through wellness content and felt like it was either written for someone twice your age or trying to sell you a set of food rules, this blog is for you. I’m a student, and I’m writing this from the middle of the exact life stage you’re probably in — classes, early alarms, big decisions, and figuring out how to take care of myself along the way.

Here’s what I believe: diet culture doesn’t work. Not for me, and I don’t think for most people. What does work is learning what makes you feel your absolute best — eating intuitively instead of by rules, moving your body to feel fit and strong, sleeping enough, and living by your own values. That combination is different for every single person.

I call it the golden spot — and finding yours is what this blog is about.

What’s coming

Mostly shorter posts: meals I actually eat, the workout classes I love (yoga and mat pilates have my heart, and so does the weight room), honest reflections on what’s been making me feel my best, and my routines — including the early mornings. Once in a while I’ll publish a longer piece called The Deep Dive: a sourced, evidence-based look at a nutrition topic or a trend that deserves some myth-busting.

Thanks for being here at the very beginning. I hope this becomes a place you come back to when you need inspiration — or just a reminder that feeling good is allowed to be enjoyable.

Everything here is personal experience plus student research — not medical or dietetic advice.
