What it is

Explain the framework simply: intuitive eating was developed in 1995 by two registered dietitians, Evelyn Tribole and Elyse Resch, and is built on ten principles — including rejecting the diet mentality, honoring hunger, and finding satisfaction in food. Summarize the principles in your own words.
