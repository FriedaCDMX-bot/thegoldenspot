Everything on **Izzy's Busy** is personal experience plus student research.

The author is a student, not a registered dietitian, doctor, therapist, or
certified trainer. Nothing on this site — including recipes, movement

mental-health, or fitness advice.

Deep Dive articles cite published sources wherever possible, but they are
summaries written by a student, not professional guidance.

If you have questions about your own nutrition, health, or training — or if
you're struggling with food or body image — please talk to a qualified
professional. If you're in the U.S. and need support with disordered eating,
the National Alliance for Eating Disorders offers a free helpline and
referrals at allianceforeatingdisorders.com.
