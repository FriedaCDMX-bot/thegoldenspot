If you've ever scrolled through wellness content and felt like it was either
written for someone twice your age or trying to sell you a set of food rules,
this blog is for you. I'm a student, and I'm writing this from the middle of
the exact life stage you're probably in — classes, early alarms, big
decisions, and figuring out how to take care of myself along the way.

Here's what I believe: diet culture doesn't work. Not for me, and I don't
think for most people. What does work is learning what makes *you* feel your
best — eating intuitively instead of by rules, moving your body to feel fit
and strong, sleeping enough, and living by your own values.

> Finding what makes you feel your best — and it's different for everyone — is what this blog is about.

Thanks for being here at the very beginning. I hope this becomes a place you
come back to when you need inspiration — or just a reminder that feeling good
is allowed to be enjoyable.
