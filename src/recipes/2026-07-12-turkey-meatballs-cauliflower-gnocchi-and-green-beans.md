I wanted a quick lunch, so I put together a few things we had on hand. All of these items are from Trader Joe's but you can make this using products from any grocery store. 



**Turkey Meatballs:**

Heaated in the microwave according to instructions on the package. Using the oven might be better, but I used the microwave for efficiency



**Cauliflower Gnocchi:**

The package offers a few heating options, but I used the "recommended" method of panfrying! I've never prepared pasta/gnocchi like this before, but I liked how the texture turned out. It was pretty easy, I just heated some oil (I opt for avocado or olive oil) on a cast iron pan and then cooked the gnocchis on each side until they were warm, soft, and crispy. 



**Green Beans:**

These are pre-cut, ready to use green beans, so I just microwaved these as well. Super easy way to get greens in
